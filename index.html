Royal Rumble Fight

Game Rules

  • Each player start the game with 100% life point and a default weapon.
  • Each weapon has an assigned value and impact
  • Each player has the ability to move up 3 steps horizontally or vertically
  • A player cannot pass through a barrier
  • When the two players are next to each other, the fight commences.
  • An attack and defense button should be displayed on the player container when the fight begins
  • When the life point of any player gets to zero or less than zero, the game ends
